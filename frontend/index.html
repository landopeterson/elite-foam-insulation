Frequently Asked Questions

Good Questions

How much does spray foam insulation cost?
It depends on the size of the area, the type of foam, and the thickness needed. We give free, no-obligation estimates — give us a call and we'll give you a clear price.
How long does the installation take?
Most residential jobs are completed in a single day. Larger commercial or new-construction projects may take longer — we'll let you know up front.
Is spray foam insulation worth it?
For most homes and buildings, yes. The energy savings and added comfort typically pay back the investment over time, and it lasts for decades.
What's the difference between open and closed cell?
Open cell is lighter, great for interior walls and sound dampening. Closed cell is denser, with a higher R-value and moisture resistance — ideal for crawl spaces and metal buildings.
How much energy can I save?
Many customers see a noticeable drop in heating and cooling costs — up to around 50% in some cases — because spray foam seals the air leaks other insulation misses.
What areas do you serve?
We serve Northeast Florida through Southeast Georgia, including Jacksonville, St. Augustine, Orange Park, and surrounding areas. Not sure if you're in range? Give us a call.
